## Overview

This portfolio showcases practical work and learning in **healthcare revenue cycle management (RCM)** with a focus on identifying and improving workflow breakdowns that lead to claim denials, rework, delays, and revenue loss.

The goal is to demonstrate **real-world understanding of how administrative workflows impact financial performance and patient access outcomes.**

## How I Approach RCM

Most revenue cycle problems do not start in billing.

They start upstream.

This portfolio is built around analyzing:

- Where workflows break
- Why errors move forward undetected
- How small intake or verification issues become denials
- How to design validation checkpoints before submission

The focus is shifting from:

**Reactive denial management**

to

**Proactive denial prevention**

## Featured Projects

### 1. Revenue Cycle Workflow Breakdown

**Problem:** Errors created during intake and eligibility often result in downstream claim failures.

**Focus:**
- Intake accuracy
- Eligibility verification
- Authorization alignment
- Documentation completeness

**Goal:** Improve clean claim rate by strengthening front-end validation.

---

### 2. Eligibility & Insurance Verification Analysis

**Problem:** Incorrect or incomplete insurance data leads to avoidable denials and delays.

**Focus:**
- Insurance data capture
- Payer verification
- Coverage validation
- Required field controls

**Goal:** Reduce eligibility-related denials and improve first-pass claim accuracy.

---

### 3. Denial Root Cause Analysis

**Problem:** Denials are treated as isolated issues instead of system failures.

**Focus:**
- Eligibility denials
- Authorization failures
- Documentation gaps
- Repeat denial patterns

**Goal:** Identify root causes and prevent repeat denial conditions.
