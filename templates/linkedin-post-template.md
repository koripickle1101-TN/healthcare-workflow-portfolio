# LinkedIn Post Template

Created by Kori Pickle

## Topic

Add the healthcare operations, RCM, risk management, patient safety, quality, or workflow topic.

## Hook

Write one sharp opening line.

Example:

Most near misses are not employee failures. They are workflow warning signals.

## Problem

Explain the operational issue in plain language.

Keep it short.

## Why It Matters

Connect the issue to one or more of the following:

- Patient safety
- Staff trust
- Revenue cycle performance
- Denial prevention
- Workflow reliability
- Quality improvement
- Risk visibility
- Leadership accountability

## System Cause

Name the workflow failure.

Examples:

- Intake gap
- Eligibility mismatch
- Documentation delay
- Handoff breakdown
- Authorization lag
- Reporting fear
- Poor feedback loop

## Better Workflow

List the improved process in 4 to 7 short lines.

## Leadership Lesson

State the strategic takeaway.

## Closing Question

End with one thoughtful question that invites discussion.
