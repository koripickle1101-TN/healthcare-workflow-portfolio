## Original Academic Topic

Paste the original discussion post, assignment paragraph, lecture notes, or healthcare concept here.

## Core Operational Insight

What is the strongest real-world healthcare operations insight?

Example:

Near-miss reporting fails when staff fear punishment more than they trust the system.

## Hook

Use one sharp opening line.

Examples:

Most near misses are not employee failures. They are workflow warning signals.

A strong safety culture does not begin with punishment. It begins with trust.

Risk management is not paperwork. It is how healthcare organizations prevent invisible harm.

A clean claim does not start in billing. It starts upstream.

## Main Breakdown

### Problem

### System Cause

### Operational Risk

### Better Workflow

### Leadership Lesson

## LinkedIn Caption Draft

Write the post in short paragraphs.

Use strong line breaks.

Remove academic filler.

Keep citations out of the LinkedIn caption unless necessary.

Save source support inside the GitHub case study instead.

## Carousel Draft

Create 5 to 7 slides.

Each slide should have one idea only.

Slide 1: Cover
Slide 2: Problem
Slide 3: Example
Slide 4: Leadership contrast
Slide 5: Framework or workflow
Slide 6: Operational lesson
Slide 7: Closing question

## Final CTA

End with one thoughtful question.

Example:

How can healthcare leaders protect accountability without making staff afraid to report near misses?
