# Design Prompts: Near Misses Are Workflow Warning Signals

Created by Kori Pickle

## Global Brand Prompt

Create a premium LinkedIn carousel slide using a feminine executive healthcare operations and RCM brand identity.

Visual style:

Pure white background #FFFFFF.
Tennessee Orange #FF8200 accent system.
Black #111111 headline text.
Deep gray #333333 supporting text.
Warm gray #E8E2DC structure lines.
High-contrast editorial serif headline.
Clean sans-serif supporting text.
40 to 50 percent whitespace.
Structured grid with intentional asymmetry.
Premium editorial healthcare strategy feel.

Design elements:

Thin Tennessee Orange top divider line.
Small centered crown icon near the top.
Double-ring circular nodes with thin orange outer stroke.
Soft interior glow inside nodes.
Dotted or faded connector lines.
Subtle warm gray workflow structure.
Minimal, clean, executive composition.

Footer:

Thin divider line above footer.
Centered text: Created by Kori Pickle.
Below it, handwritten cursive signature: Kori Pickle.
Signature should look elegant, feminine, smooth, slightly right slanted, with natural ink variation in black or dark gray.
Below signature, add minimal LinkedIn and GitHub glyph icons in black or deep gray.

Do not overcrowd the slide.
Do not use stock-photo medical imagery.
Do not make it look like a school assignment.
Make it look like an executive healthcare operations insight.
